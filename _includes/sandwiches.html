The wedding will be catered by Zingerman's, an Ann Arbor institution that is famous for its delicious deli sandwiches. President Obama recently described their rueben as "killer," and Food & Wine has named it one of the best sandwiches in the country.

We're going to offer three different Zingerman's sandwiches for dinner, and we need to know which one you'd like.

The first is called "Sherman's Sure Choice." It's a slight twist on the classic reuben, with coleslaw instead of sauerkraut.

Sherman's Sure Choice

Zingerman’s corned beef, Switzerland Swiss cheese, coleslaw & Russian dressing on Jewish rye bread.

The second offering is a chicken salad sandwich called "Mary's Commute." It's a little lighter, though equally delicious.

Mary's Commute

Zingerman's delicious chicken salad (chunks of hand-cut Amish chicken, mayo, onion & celery), Nueske’s applewood-smoked bacon, lettuce & mayo on challah bread.

And, finally, the third option is for you vegetarians and Italian sandwich lovers. It's called "Gemini Rocks the House."

Gemini Rocks the House

Zingerman’s own handmade fresh mozzarella, tomatoes & Italian pesto on grilled farm bread.
